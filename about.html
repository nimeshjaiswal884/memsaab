About US

MEM SAAB is a premium ethnic wear label based in Malad, Mumbai. Our atelier specializes in handcrafted festive and occasion wear designed for the modern Indian woman.

Each collection blends traditional craftsmanship with contemporary silhouettes inspired by Indian royalty.

We supply both retail customers and boutique partners across India through our B2B wholesale network.

The Essence

At MEM SAAB, every piece reflects a balance of tradition and modern elegance. Our designs celebrate Indian heritage while embracing contemporary silhouettes suited for today’s women.

From fabric selection to embroidery and finishing, each step is crafted with care to deliver a truly luxurious experience.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Heritage-Inspired Craftsmanship, Tailored for Today

At MEM SAAB, we celebrate refined Indian femininity through heritage-inspired craftsmanship and modern tailoring.
