Our Story

Where heritage craft meets the modern Indian woman.

MEM SAAB is a premium ethnic wear label rooted in Mumbai. We craft handmade festive and occasion wear that blends traditional artistry with contemporary silhouettes.

The Essence

Tradition, Refined

At MEM SAAB, every piece reflects a careful balance of tradition and modern elegance. Our designs celebrate Indian heritage while embracing contemporary silhouettes suited for today's women.

From fabric selection to embroidery and finishing, each step is carried out with meticulous care — delivering a truly luxurious experience that begins the moment you hold the garment.
